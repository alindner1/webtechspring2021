About Me!

Hi my name is Tootoo! I’m told I was named Tootoo because it was a family joke that went too far. My family calls me Harvey at the dog park though. I think it’s because they find my name ridiculous and embarrassing. I like my name though, I feel like it suits me well.

Some fun facts about me: I am a 2-year-old maltipoo (maltese poodle mix), and I live in Wilton, CT. I love playing ball, running around outside, going for walks, visiting the dog park, and eating bones!

Every morning, I wake up, go out, eat breakfast, play ball, and eat my bone to start my day off right. Throughout the day, I like to play with my toys! Toys are very VERY important to me. I have a pretty large collection.

I have a lot of toys I throw around the house and hide in my humans’ beds. Since I am clearly so passionate about my toys, I figured I should make a page with reviews of my favorite toys and places to go play.

If you are a dog, sit and stay! If you are a human, you can sit and stay and learn what your dogs want to do and what toys they should be given (you're welcome!).
