FAVORITE SPOTS

Wilton, CT

This is one of my favorite places ever - my home!

I love wandering the front and back yards of my house, jumping on our couch in the den, and going for walks around the Wilton area.

I spend most of my time in Wilton, and I absolutely love it here. We have so many trees and branches and sticks oh my! There are also so many dogs in Wilton. Every time I step foot out of my front door I see a doggie or another animal to play with (except squirrels they are not my friends).

I would highly recommend you come visit Wilton and take a nice long walk on the Wilton roads or in the Wilton Town Center for some nice fresh green air!

These are some of my favorite spots!
