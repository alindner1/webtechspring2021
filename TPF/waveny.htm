FAVORITE SPOTS

Waveny Park - New Caanan, CT

When I am extra extra good, I get to go to Waveny Park!

I really like this park a lot! I enjoy peeing on every single tree here and sniffing as many puppy butts as I can.


Waveny Park is especially special as I've only been a few times and it's so pretty. They have a beautiful trail and a nice castle looking building in the center.

Check it out if you're looking for an interesting clientele of dogs (I saw the weirdest poodle when I went there last time) and a nice hogwarts feel.

9.5/10 would recommend to a friend and would go back.
